# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, and publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
maintainers via a private GitHub Security Advisory or by contacting the repository
owner. Maintainers will review and respond, and may remove, edit, or reject
contributions or comments that violate this Code of Conduct.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
